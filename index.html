What We Do

Assessment & Study Services

We provide specialized electrical study and assessment services for facilities that need thorough, expert analysis from an experienced independent engineer.

01

Hazardous Area Classification

Facilities change. Documentation doesn't always keep up. We provide thorough NEC 500/505 and NFPA 70 hazardous area classification studies — reviewing process documentation, evaluating existing classifications, and delivering a complete report with clear, actionable findings. Whether you need an initial classification, a reclassification following process changes, or an independent review of existing documentation, we provide the rigorous analysis your facility requires.

02

Grounding & Lightning Studies

Grounding systems are foundational to electrical safety — and frequently underexamined until something goes wrong. We assess grounding systems and lightning installations for industrial, commercial, and military facilities, evaluating existing conditions against applicable standards such as IEEE 80, and delivering thorough, clearly documented findings.

03

Electrical Condition Assessments

Aging infrastructure, deferred maintenance, unfamiliar inherited systems — electrical condition assessments give facility owners and operators an expert picture of electrical system health. We document existing conditions, identify concerns, and provide prioritized findings you can act on, share with stakeholders, or use as the basis for future planning. On your timeline.
